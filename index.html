Care

Help us provide basic necessities (food, water, living essentials/medical amenities) to Yemen during the month of Ramadan. This money will be sent directly to the hands of families and individuals who are suffering from the impact of flash flooding, famine, and war. During this holy month, we aim to raise at least $2,500 to provide assistance to those in need in Yemen.

Campaign Story

On April 21, 2020, flash floods hit the city of Aden in Yemen, killing at least 21 people. For the past five years, Yemen has been wracked by a bloody war, and the armed conflict in Yemen has resulted in the largest humanitarian crisis in the world.

Due to the conflict, thousands of Yemeni civilians have been killed and injured. According to the Yemen Data Project, more than 17,500 civilians were killed and injured since 2015, and a quarter of all civilians killed in air raids were women and children. More than 20 million people in Yemen are experiencing food insecurity; 10 million of them are at risk of famine.

What makes our campaign unique is our personal connection to those affected. We have been contacted by friends and family in the city of Aden with constant updates on issues faced by the community. We intend on providing this financial support to the people of Aden, Yemen, who are in need of our help.
